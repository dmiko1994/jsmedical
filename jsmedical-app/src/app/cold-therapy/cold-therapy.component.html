The Benefits of Cold Therapy

Why Cold Therapy?

  • The temperature of the tissue is lowered and this results in reducing the inflammation of the affected area. This promotes faster healing time
  • The cold also acts as an analgesic and reduces the pain caused by the inflammation and swelling
  • Cold Therapy can reduce inflammation, prevent swelling, and mitigate pain in the joint space after surgery

Non-Pharma Pain Management

  • Utilizing cold therapy can help decrease the amount of opioids prescribed after surgery
  • In a study, it was found that the cold therapy group consumed significantly lesser amounts of opioids (47 tablets) as compared to the group without cold therapy (83 tablets)
