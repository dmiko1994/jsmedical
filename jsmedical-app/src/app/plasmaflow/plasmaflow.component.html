PlasmaFlow

The PlasmaFlow is intended to be an easy to use sequential compression system, prescribed by a physician, for use in the home or clinical setting to help prevent the onset of DVT in patients by stimulating blood flow in the extremities (simulating muscle contractions).

  • Portable, lightweight and tubeless
  • Long lasting rechargeable battery. Up to 10 hours of use on one charge
  • 2 Modes: Slow inflation and Step-Up technology which allows the unit to increase pressure in slower increments
  • 2 LCD screens to monitor usage and pressure
