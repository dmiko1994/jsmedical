Our Cause

At JS Medical, we want to thank our veterans for their service and sacrifice by giving back to our communities through various philanthropic events.

A portion of all our proceeds are donated to military philanthropic organizations. We are proud to be active donors to the Army Fisher House and we are honored to give back to our military families. We are passionate about making a positive change in our veterans lives!
