Cryo Knee

The ManaEZ ROM is a quick fitting, universal, knee range of motion orthosis. The brace includes range of motion hinges to allow for flexion and extension control. A quick inflation system that can improve outcomes by managing pain and swelling. This knee brace also includes an ice pack.

  • Removeable range of motion hinges
  • Removable ice pack
  • Compression bulb to fill the knee orthosis with air
  • Lightweight, latex free breathable material
  • Easy application, universal size with hook and buckle closure
  • Designed for either left or right leg
