Cryo Shoulder

The Cryo Shoulder is a convenient shoulder orthosis for pain, swelling, and to compress the shoulder joint.

  • Pump up with air and compress the cold to alleviate pain faster
  • Removable gel liner for cooling in freezer
  • Drip free fabric to allow breathing
  • Easy to apply over clothing
  • Can be used for sprains, sports injuries, rehab, arthritis, and muscle tensisons/fatigues
